Our History

A Passion That Spans Generations

The Passion for Wine

Our story began with a simple dream: to produce wines that capture the essence of the land and the passion of those who create them. Since its founding, the owners of Premium Winery have dedicated their lives to cultivating not just grapes, but also a tradition of excellence that is reflected in every bottle.

The love for wine transcends generations in our family. It is a passion that drives us daily, from dawn in the vineyards to the moment each bottle is carefully labeled. We believe that making wine is an art that combines ancestral knowledge, tireless dedication and deep respect for nature.

The Douro Region

Located in the heart of the Douro demarcated region, a UNESCO World Heritage site, our winery benefits from a unique and privileged terroir. The steep slopes sculpted in terraces over centuries provide ideal conditions for cultivating grapes of exceptional quality.

The Mediterranean climate, with hot, dry summers and mild winters, combined with poor but mineral-rich schist soils, creates the perfect balance. It is in this challenging environment that our vines develop deep roots, extracting from the earth the complexity and intensity that characterize our wines.

Excellence in Every Bunch

Excellence begins in the vineyard. Each vine is treated with individual attention, respecting its natural rhythm and unique characteristics. We practice sustainable and integrated viticulture, where human intervention works in harmony with nature's cycles.

Traditional Portuguese grape varieties ‚Äì Touriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz and many others ‚Äì are cultivated with methods that favor quality over quantity. Manual harvesting, carried out at the ideal point of maturation, ensures that only the best grapes reach the cellar.

From Earth to Glass

The winemaking process is where science meets art. In our cellars, equipped with modern technology but guided by traditional principles, each stage is carefully monitored. From fermentation to aging in French oak barrels, every decision is made with one goal: to preserve and enhance the unique characteristics of each wine.

When the wine is finally served, it carries with it an entire story ‚Äì the story of the land that nourished it, the hands that cultivated it, and the passion that inspired it. It is this commitment to excellence, passed from generation to generation, that makes each bottle from Premium Winery an authentic and unforgettable experience.
