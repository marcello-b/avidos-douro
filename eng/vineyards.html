The Douro Terroir

Our vineyards are located in the heart of the Douro Demarcated Region, recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site. The steep slopes, sculpted into terraces over centuries, create a unique landscape where exceptional grapes are born.

Climate and Soil

The Mediterranean climate with hot, dry summers and mild winters, combined with schist soils poor in organic matter but rich in minerals, creates ideal conditions for growing high-quality grapes. These challenging conditions force the vines to develop deep roots, resulting in wines of exceptional complexity and intensity.

Sustainable Viticulture

We practice integrated and sustainable viticulture, respecting nature's cycles and minimizing environmental impact. Each vine receives individual care, and manual harvesting at the ideal point of maturation ensures that only the best grapes reach the cellar.

Traditional Grape Varieties

We cultivate the region's traditional Portuguese grape varieties, including Touriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta Barroca and many others. These indigenous varieties, perfectly adapted to the Douro terroir, are the soul of our wines.

Heritage and Innovation

We balance respect for tradition with the adoption of modern techniques. Our century-old vineyards coexist with new plantings, and ancient knowledge combines with enological science to produce wines that honor the past while looking to the future.

Producing exceptional wines for over 70 years with passion, tradition and excellence.
