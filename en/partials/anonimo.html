Anônimo

A tribute to the Roman Empire officer who lived his passion for the maiden.


Wine Specifications by vintage


TASTING NOTES

Displays an intense ruby color, with aromas of ripe black and red fruits. Toasted notes and spices derived from aging in new French oak barrels. A fuller-bodied wine, more elegant, with remarkable persistence. A typical expression of the Douro.


WINE SPECIFICATIONS

Region: Douro
Sub-region: Douro Superior – Torre de Moncorvo
Soil type: Predominantly schist
Vineyards: Salgueiras and Sardoal
Age of vineyards: 15 to 30 years
Grape varieties: Tinta Roriz, Touriga Franca, and Sousão
Planting density per ha: 4,400 vines
Vineyard altitude: 250 to 500 m
Harvest period: September
Fermentation: Stainless steel tanks
Bottled: July 2018
Aging: 22 months in new French oak barrels
Alcohol: 13.5% vol
Production: 3,000 bottles
