Ávidos

A fervent, ardent, and unrestrained desire for something or someone!


The winery’s icon, produced only in exceptional vintages, with a great diversity of grape varieties. The result is a highly elegant and complex wine, in the style of the finest old vines.

Wine Specifications by vintage


TASTING NOTES

Ruby-colored, with aromas of red and wild berries, complemented by spice notes from aging in first-use French oak. Notable for the softness and silkiness of its tannins. The 2014 vintage shows a bit more body.


WINE SPECIFICATIONS

Region: Douro
Sub-region: Douro Superior – Torre de Moncorvo
Soil type: Predominantly schist
Vineyards: Sardoal
Age of vineyards: 15 to 30 years
Grape varieties: Tinta Amarela, Touriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta Barroca, Tinta Francisca, Tinto Cão, Sousão, and Marufo
Planting density per ha: 4,400 vines
Vineyard altitude: 250 to 500 m
Harvest period: September
Fermentation: Stainless steel tanks
Bottled: July 2019
Aging: 30 months in first-use French oak barrels
Alcohol: 13.6% vol
Production: 3,300 bottles
