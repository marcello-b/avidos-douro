A toast to all lovers!


Wine Specifications by vintage

TASTING NOTES

Rosé-colored wine, with wild red berries, raspberry, and cherry. A fresh, dry wine with good acidity. Seductive and passionate finish.


WINE SPECIFICATIONS

Region: Douro
Sub-region: Douro Superior – Torre de Moncorvo
Soil type: Predominantly schist
Vineyards: Salgueiras and Sardoal
Age of vineyards: 15 to 30 years
Grape variety: Touriga Nacional
Planting density per ha: 4,400 vines
Vineyard altitude: 250 to 500 m
Harvest period: Early September
Fermentation: Stainless steel tanks with automatic temperature control
Bottled: December 2020
Aging: 16 months in second-use French oak barrels
Alcohol: 12.5% vol
Production: 2,800 bottles
