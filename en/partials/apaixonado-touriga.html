Apaixonado Touriga

A toast to all lovers!


Wine Specifications by vintage


TASTING NOTES

Intense in both color and aroma, with very ripe red fruits and some black fruits. Slightly toasted notes from barrel aging. Full-bodied, with present yet polished tannins. Long, silky finish. Highly persistent.


INFORMAÇÃO TÉCNICA

Region: Douro
Sub-region: Douro Superior – Torre de Moncorvo
Soil type: Predominantly schist
Vineyards: Salgueiras and Sardoal
Age of vineyards: 15 to 25 years
Grape variety: Touriga Nacional (3 vineyard plots)
Planting density per ha: 4,400 vines
Vineyard altitude: 250 to 500 m
Harvest period: Late August, early September
Fermentation: Stainless steel tanks with pump-over system and automatic temperature control
Bottled: July 2020
Aging: 30 months in French oak barrels, 2nd to 4th use
Alcohol: 14.5% vol
Production: 2,000 bottles
