Apaixonado Touriga Franca

A toast to all lovers!


Wine Specifications by vintage


TASTING NOTES

Clear, deep ruby in color. The nose shows ripe red fruit aromas well integrated with subtle spice and toasted notes derived from oak aging. On the palate, the red fruit character carries through, with a good balance between acidity, tannins, and alcohol, resulting in a smooth wine with a long, persistent finish.
