Our Olive Oil

Our extra-virgin, produced on a small scale for tables that demand quality.

Cupido

The god who inspires love and passion, bringing happiness to couples.


TASTING NOTES

Reveals a ripe fruity aroma, with notes of green tomato, fresh leaves, and subtle hints of dried fruits. On the palate, it is very lightly bitter and mildly spicy, with a smooth and persistent flavor.


TECHNICAL INFORMATION

Origin: Douro
Category: Extra-virgin
Olive varieties: Cobrançosa, Cordovil, Madural, and Verdeal
Soil: Schist
Altitude: between 200 and 500 m
Extraction: Cold mechanical extraction, preserving the fresh aromas of the olives
Storage: Keep protected from light and heat sources
Shelf life: By law, 2 years after bottling. However, this type of olive oil may be consumed up to 3–4 years without losing its main qualitative characteristics
Acidity: 0.11%
Gluten: Does not contain
Packaging: 500 ml
Case: 6 bottles
