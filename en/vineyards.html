Our Vineyards

Where Tradition Meets Excellence

A Terroir of Distinction

We cultivate a total of around 17 hectares across two vineyards. Sardoal, located by the Douro River at altitudes above 150 meters, produces grapes of greater concentration. Salgueiras, reaching up to 550 meters, yields fresher grapes with higher acidity.

Our vineyards, aged between 15 and 30 years, are all planted by grape variety. This allows for separate harvests and vinifications, ensuring the best expression of each variety. The diversity of grapes and altitudes provides more balanced blends, vintage after vintage.

The final result is wines of remarkable quality—surprising, elegant, and true to the Douro’s tradition of excellence.
