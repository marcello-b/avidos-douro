Our History

A Passion That Crosses Generations

The Passion for Wine

Lovers of fine wine, we found in the Douro Superior—amid its legends and traditions—the perfect conditions for producing great wines. They are capable of awakening pleasures and special emotions in every tasting. Thus began our dream, with the acquisition of two estates: Quinta do Sardoal and Quinta das Salgueiras, both in Torre de Moncorvo. From there come some of the region’s most distinguished wines.

The Douro Region

Located in the heart of the Douro Demarcated Region, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, our winery benefits from a unique and privileged terroir. The steep slopes, sculpted into terraces over centuries, provide ideal conditions for cultivating grapes of exceptional quality.

The Mediterranean climate—with hot, dry summers and mild winters—combined with poor yet mineral-rich schist soils, creates the perfect balance. In this challenging environment, our vines develop deep roots, drawing from the earth the complexity and intensity that define our wines.

Excellence in Every Cluster

Excellence begins in the vineyard. Each vine is cared for individually, respecting its natural rhythm and unique characteristics. We practice sustainable and integrated viticulture, where human intervention works in harmony with nature’s cycles.

Traditional Portuguese grape varieties — Touriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, among many others—are cultivated with methods that prioritize quality over quantity. Manual harvesting, carried out at the ideal point of ripeness, ensures that only the finest grapes reach the winery.

The Legend That Inspires Us

Legend tells that, in the early days of Lusitania, a young maiden fell deeply in love with a high officer of the Roman Empire. Her family, disapproving of the romance, took her beyond the Douro River. There, on the right bank in the Douro Superior, she lived among the vineyards, wandering day and night, immersed in tears for her beloved.

Her tears, filled with desire and passion, soaked into the schist soils of the vineyards, creating a unique terroir for wines that foster understanding among people and awaken love.

From this land come ÁVIDOS, our premium wine; ANÓNIMO, our finest expression of traditional Douro red; APAIXONADO, in red, white, and rosé—soft, charming, and captivating; and AMÁVIO, an ancient Portuguese word meaning “elixir of love and seduction.” These are balanced wines, easy to drink, yet capable of stirring powerful emotions.
